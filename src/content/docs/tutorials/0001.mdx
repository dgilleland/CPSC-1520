You can start with the [**JavaScript Quickstart**](#javascript-quickstart) for beginners followed by the series [**About Objects and Functions**](#about-objects-and-functions). From there, you can jump into either [JavaScript Projects](#javascript-projects) or [Flow Control Statements](#flow-control-statements).
